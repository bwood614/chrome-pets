Net Pets Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 28, 2025

Net Pets is a Chrome extension designed to enhance your browsing experience by adding virtual pets to your browser.

Net Pets does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

  • We do not use forms to request information from users.
  • We do not track browsing activity, capture website content, or access any personally identifiable information.
  • We do not use cookies or other technologies to identify or track users.

Since Net Pets does not handle any user data, there are no additional data protection measures required under Google's User Data Policy.
